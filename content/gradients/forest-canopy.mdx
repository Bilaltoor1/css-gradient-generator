# Forest Canopy Gradient

Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of nature with this Forest Canopy gradient. Perfect for eco-friendly designs and nature-inspired projects.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Forest** (#0d5016) - Rich, dark green
- **Forest Green** (#228B22) - Classic natural green
- **Light Green** (#90EE90) - Fresh, bright accent

## Usage Examples

### CSS Implementation

```css
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #0d5016 0%, #228B22 50%, #90EE90 100%);
```
